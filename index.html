About Me

I am an MPhil student in Machine Learning and Machine Intelligence at the University of Cambridge. I recently graduated King's College London with a BSc in Computer Science (Artificial Intelligence).

I am a research visitor at Systems Security Research Lab (S2lab), supervised by Professor Lorenzo Cavallaro, Dr. Fabio Pierazzi and Dr. Feargus Pendlebury.

My general research interest is focused on understanding the behaviour of machine learning systems in adversarial environments.
