About Me

I am a DPhil (PhD) student in Computer Science at the University of Oxford, supervised by Professor Michael Bronstein. My research is in the field of Geometric Deep Learning. I hold an MPhil in Machine Learning and Machine Intelligence from the University of Cambridge, during which I worked on Geometric Deep Learning, supervised by Professor Pietro Liò and Cristian Bodnar. I graduated King's College London with a BSc in Computer Science (Artificial Intelligence). I was a research visitor at Systems Security Research Lab (S2lab), supervised by Professor Lorenzo Cavallaro, Dr. Fabio Pierazzi and Dr. Feargus Pendlebury.
