Hi! I'm a PhD student in Computer Science at the University of Oxford (Trinity College), supervised by Michael Bronstein. My research is in the area of Geometric Deep Learning and I also have a broad interest in ML security, robustness and privacy. I am currently at Google DeepMind in London working with Petar Veličković on Algorithmic Reasoning.

In 2023, I interned at Microsoft Research in Amsterdam on the AI4Science team working on protein sampling. In 2022, I did my MPhil in Machine Learning and Machine Intelligence at the University of Cambridge (King's College), supervised by Pietro Liò and Cristian Bodnar working on Topological Deep Learning.

I am always happy to meet new people! Feel free to contact me either on Twitter or via email.
